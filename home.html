Who I am?

Hi! I’m Angela, I was born in Barranquilla, the coolest corner of Colombia. actually I’m a graphic designer in progress. A few years ago I started my path as a photographer, capturing the best landscapes and lovely animals. After that, I fell in love with the portrait style and now I decided to show the beauty of each people. I also enjoy to freeze happy moments for others. Hope you like it!
